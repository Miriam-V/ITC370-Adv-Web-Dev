Class Descriptions

Power Box: Boxing and martial arts-inspired workouts for strength and stress relief.

Spinning Spectrum: Indoor cycling classes with varying intensities for endurance and leg strength.

Strength Sculpt: Resistance training and weightlifting classes for muscle building and toning.

Aquafit Oasis: Low-impact aquatic fitness classes for joint-friendly exercise and core strength.

Senior Strength: Tailored classes for older adults focusing on strength, balance, and mobility.

Infinity HIIT: High-Intensity Interval Training for maximum calorie burn and endurance.

Nutrition Workshop: Educational sessions on healthy eating and meal planning for overall well-being

Dance Fusion: High-energy dance workouts combining various dance styles for fun and fitness.



Staff

Mike Rodriguez

Mike is a Certified Boxing Coach and a seasoned MMA practitioner with over 15 years of experience in combat sports. He has trained both amateurs and professionals, instilling discipline and self-confidence in his students. Mike's classes not only focus on physical conditioning but also provide practical self-defense skills. His energy and passion for boxing and martial arts are contagious, making every class an exciting and empowering experience.

Emily Turner

Emily is a Certified Group Fitness Instructor (AFAA) and a dedicated Spinning Instructor. With a background in endurance sports, she brings a wealth of knowledge to her cardio-focused classes. Emily is known for her motivating and high-energy teaching style. Her classes are designed to challenge participants while ensuring they have a blast during their workouts.

David Clark

David is a Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist (CSCS) and a Sports Nutritionist with a master's degree in Exercise Science. He has worked with athletes at various levels, from high school to professional, and has helped clients achieve their fitness and performance goals. In addition to teaching strength training classes, David conducts informative and engaging nutrition workshops to educate members on the importance of fueling their bodies for success.

Linda Mitchell

Linda is a Certified AquaFit Instructor and a Certified Senior Fitness Specialist (CSFS). She has a deep understanding of the unique fitness needs of older adults and individuals seeking low-impact workouts. Linda's classes in AquaFit Oasis and Senior Strength provide a safe and enjoyable environment for members to improve their overall fitness and well-being.

Max Sanchez

Max is a Certified HIIT Trainer and a Certified Dance Fitness Instructor. As a former competitive athlete, he knows the value of pushing boundaries and achieving fitness goals. Max's classes in Infinity HIIT and Dance Fusion are dynamic and energetic, encouraging participants to reach their maximum potential while having fun.
